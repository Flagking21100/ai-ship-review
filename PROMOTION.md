## Release post

I built AI Ship Review, a Codex skill for reviewing whether AI-built apps are actually ready to ship.

It checks the gap between "it runs locally" and "I can responsibly put this in front of users": secrets, env config, auth, payments, data, deploys, tests, rollback, and operational basics.

It outputs a ship decision, a readiness score, P0/P1 risks with evidence, and the exact artifacts to create before launch.
